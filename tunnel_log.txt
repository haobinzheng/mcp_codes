./start_ai_tool_adk_tunnel
Usage: ./start_ai_tool_adk_tunnel user@linux-host.example

Mac (this machine): forwards your browser to ADK Web on the Linux host.

  1) On the Linux server (repo checkout):
       export GEMINI_API_KEY=...
       ./start_ai_tool_adk
     (Listens on 127.0.0.1:8787 by default; see ADK_WEB_HOST / ADK_WEB_PORT.)

  2) On this Mac, run (leave the terminal open):
       ./start_ai_tool_adk_tunnel user@linux-host.example

  3) Open in Safari/Chrome on the Mac:
       http://127.0.0.1:8787
     Pick app **gfiber_network** in the ADK Web UI.

Overrides: REMOTE_SSH_TARGET=user@host  LOCAL_PORT=8787  REMOTE_PORT=8787  REMOTE_HOST=127.0.0.1
Optional: START_AI_TOOL_TUNNEL_OPEN_BROWSER=1  → run \`open\` on macOS after the tunnel starts.
